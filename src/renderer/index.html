Multi-Kill Clipper

How to Use This App
  1. Make sure your League of Legends client is open.
  2. Enter any summoner name in your region.
  3. Select the multi-kill types you wish to view.
  4. Multi-Kill Clipper will automatically detect any multi-kills of the selected type, as long as they have occured on the current patch.
  5. From the search results, Select the multi-kill you would like to clip and ensure the lane role is correct. The pre-selected role is not 100% accurate, and may wrong if the player is autofilled or they lane swap.
  6. Once 'Create Clip' is pressed, Multi-Kill Clipper will download and launch the replay, skip to the time of the kill sequence, and begin recording.
  7. Once rendering completes, the clip can be found in your Leage of Legends Highlights folder.
  8. You can change the file path your highlights are saved to by navigating to the "In-Game/Replays" tab in the League Client's settings menu.
